== Notes ==
*This song can be obtained either by purchasing the Challenge Pass and finishing all Event Challenges during the [[Limited Events#Conflicting Powers|Conflicting Powers Limited Event]], or by purchasing it with [[Conflict|conflict]] through the Event Shop (when available) for 800 Chiming Cores.

==Trivia==
* This song is also available in Chrono Circle and [https://sixtargate.fandom.com/wiki/Conflict_-NOMA_CONCEIVER_REMIX- Sixtar Gate: STARTRAIL].
* The character in this song's cover art also appears on the art of [[Brain Power]].
* Despite only being unlockable before [[Conflict|conflict]], this song appears after it in [[Tracklist Mode]] (similar to [[Malicious Mischance]]).
